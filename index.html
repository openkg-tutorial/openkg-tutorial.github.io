Open-Environment Knowledge Graph Construction and Reasoning:
Challenges, Approaches, and Opportunitie

Lecture Tutorial for The IJCAI 2023



Abstract

This tutorial provides an extensive and comparative overview of open-environment knowledge graph construction, as developing intelligent systems capable of extracting, representing, and reasoning in open and dynamic environments is critical for enabling data-driven decision-making and optimizing complex processes. The tutorial encompasses the background, benchmarks, challenges, approaches and opportunities related to this field.
